# hymn_urgent

**Purpose:** Theology of terror dominates — hymns sung so as not to be noticed.

**Style:** dungeon synth (whole soundtrack shares one palette: tape-warm synths,
medieval modality, lo-fi patina; no vocals with words, no modern drums).

**Suno.ai prompt:**

> Dungeon synth hymn, tense and imploring, 84 BPM. Minor-key organ ostinato, hushed staccato choir, insistent low tom pulse, dissonant suspensions that never fully resolve, claustrophobic close reverb. Worship as appeasement. Instrumental, loopable, 2-3 minutes.
