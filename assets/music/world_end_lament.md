# world_end_lament

**Purpose:** Total extinction — the hold on the empty world and the Chronicle screen.

**Style:** dungeon synth (whole soundtrack shares one palette: tape-warm synths,
medieval modality, lo-fi patina; no vocals with words, no modern drums).

**Suno.ai prompt:**

> Dungeon synth elegy, very slow, 50 BPM. Solo distant horn-like lead over a single sustained drone, sparse funeral bell every few bars, tape flutter, long silences between phrases. Grief without drama; the hearths have gone out. Instrumental, 2-4 minutes, may end rather than loop.
