# rite_melody

**Purpose:** A rite has crystallized — the first shared ritual music of the culture.

**Style:** dungeon synth (whole soundtrack shares one palette: tape-warm synths,
medieval modality, lo-fi patina; no vocals with words, no modern drums).

**Suno.ai prompt:**

> Dungeon synth, primitive and ceremonial, 100 BPM. Repetitive chanted-feeling synth motif of 4 notes, frame-drum heartbeat, drone in open fifths, slightly out-of-tune ensemble feel like villagers learning a song. Hypnotic, earthen. Instrumental, loopable, 90 seconds to 2 minutes.
