# season_winter

**Purpose:** In-run bed, season 3 — scarcity, hearths, the long dark.

**Style:** dungeon synth (whole soundtrack shares one palette: tape-warm synths,
medieval modality, lo-fi patina; no vocals with words, no modern drums).

**Suno.ai prompt:**

> Dungeon synth, sparse and cold, 60 BPM. Icy glassy pad, very slow single-note lead with long tails, sub-bass drone, wind-like noise swells, huge empty reverb. Phrygian touches, dread kept at arm's length by a small warm motif. Instrumental, loopable, 2-3 minutes.
